☀️ Solar

⚡ LONGi pushes silicon-perovskite tandem cell to a record 35.5%

LONGi's tandem team lifted its crystalline silicon-perovskite cell to 35.5% efficiency, certified by Europe's ESTI — the latest step in a run from 33.9% in 2023 to 34.6% in 2024. Tandem modules already hit 31.4% and 29.4%, showing the tech is edging toward commercial scale against a theoretical ceiling near 43%.

Source: CleanTechnica

Cypress Creek breaks ground on a 2.5 GW solar-plus-storage build-out

Developer Cypress Creek started construction on a project set to deliver 2.5 GW of solar generation paired with 2.9 GWh of battery storage. It's a reminder that utility-scale pipelines keep advancing even as US residential rooftop softens.

Source: Canary Media

US residential rooftop set for its weakest year since 2020

Home solar installs are forecast to fall to their lowest level in six years, largely due to the removal of federal tax incentives from January 2026. Commercial rooftop and community-benefit models are picking up some of the slack.

Source: Utility Dive

🔋 Battery Technology

⚡ CATL bets sodium-ion will take over grid storage

CATL is rolling out 5 GWh of its Tener sodium-ion batteries, pitching a 15,000-cycle life and wide-temperature durability as the answer for grid storage where lithium's energy density isn't essential. The firm expects cumulative shipments to reach 1 GWh by year-end.

Source: Electrek

Peak Energy plans a 4 GWh sodium-ion factory near Sacramento

GM-backed Peak Energy will build a $71m, 17,000 m² plant producing 4 GWh of sodium-ion cells a year — enough, it says, to power 4 million homes. Peak believes it can crack the US market where earlier sodium startups stumbled.

Source: SodiumBatteryHub

NUS team clears a hurdle to safe all-solid-state sodium batteries

Researchers led by Palani Balaya at the National University of Singapore used a single low-cost additive to overcome a key barrier, opening a scalable route to safe, cheap solid-state sodium cells for both grid and EV use.

Source: Tech Xplore

Bottom line

Sodium is having a moment: CATL and GM-backed Peak Energy are both scaling grid batteries around cycle life and cost rather than density, while LONGi's 35.5% tandem cell shows silicon-perovskite solar still has headroom. Storage is the connecting theme of the day — pumped hydro just crossed 200 GW globally, with fresh money flowing in Spain and Morocco. Fusion pulled in a record $4.48bn over the past year and SMRs edged toward market with Holtec's IPO filing, though both remain years from meaningful grid power. The clearest policy signal is divergence: US federal support for rooftop solar and heat pumps has been pulled, yet states, the EU and utilities are filling the gap, and adoption is holding up. Watch ocean energy — IRENA's first cost assessment is the credibility milestone wave and tidal have been waiting for.
