☀️ Solar

⚡ Longi sets 35.5% world record for perovskite-silicon tandem cell

Longi announced a certified 35.5% power-conversion efficiency for a two-terminal crystalline-silicon/perovskite tandem cell at its innovation conference on 14 July, beating its own 34.85% record from April 2025. The result was verified by the EU Joint Research Centre's ESTI lab, though Longi says it has no mass-production plan yet — large-area deposition, yield and stability still need work.

Researchers reported a perovskite triple-junction cell reaching 27.3% efficiency alongside a record 770 hours of operational stability — notable because durability, not headline efficiency, remains the technology's main barrier to commercialisation. Mainstream perovskite tandems for homes are still expected around 2027–2028 as manufacturing scales.

🔋 Battery Technology

⚡ Peak Energy to build 4-GWh sodium-ion factory in Sacramento

Peak Energy Technologies said on 9 July it will invest up to $71 million in a grid-scale sodium-ion battery factory in Sacramento, producing up to 4 GWh of storage systems a year for utility and commercial projects. Production is slated to start in Q1 2027 — a sign sodium-ion is moving from lab to US manufacturing at scale.

From 21 July 2026 the Boiler Upgrade Scheme grant increases from £7,500 to £9,000 for homes heated by oil or LPG — covering some 1.7 million households — to accelerate fuel-switching in off-gas-grid properties. The 0% VAT relief on heat pumps, solar and batteries also runs until 31 March 2027.
