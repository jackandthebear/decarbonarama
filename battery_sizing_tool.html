How we calculated this

Suggested battery products

Methodology: LFP 90% usable depth of discharge assumed throughout. TOU/Go tariff adds a full-day coverage requirement (battery must store enough cheap-rate electricity to last until next charge window). Evening peak fraction from typical UK half-hourly demand profiles. Heat pump peak fraction reflects that well-commissioned heat pumps run primarily during mild daytime hours with weather compensation — only a portion of daily electricity is needed at peak tariff times. All recommendations rounded up to nearest 0.5 kWh and then to nearest product size.
