⛽ Gas Consumption & Boiler

Your annual gas consumption is the foundation of the calculation. Look at last year's gas bill for the total kWh figure — or use your smart meter data. This is the single most important number you'll enter.

5,000 = small flat · 15,000 = average house · 30,000+ = large house
